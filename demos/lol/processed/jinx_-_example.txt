# Jinx
Title: The Loose Cannon
Roles: Marksman
Region: Zaun
Faction: None (Independent)
Resource: Mana
Attack Range: 525

## Lore
Jinx is a chaotic and impulsive criminal from Zaun. She creates mayhem for amusement and delights 
in destruction. Her rivalry with Vi fuels many conflicts in Piltover and Zaun. She is known for 
her unpredictability, manic personality, and mastery of explosives.

## Abilities
Switcheroo! (Q): Jinx switches between her minigun and rocket launcher.
Zap! (W): Jinx fires a long-range shock blast.
Flame Chompers! (E): Jinx throws snare grenades that root enemies.
Super Mega Death Rocket! (R): Jinx fires a global rocket that gains damage with travel distance.

## Relationships
Jinx is the primary rival of Vi. She also has connections to Caitlyn and Ekko through Zaunite and Piltover conflicts.

## Moves (summary)
Jinx uses long-range attacks, alternating between sustained DPS and explosive burst damage.
