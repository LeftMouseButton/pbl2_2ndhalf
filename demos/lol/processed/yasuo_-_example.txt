# Yasuo
Title: The Unforgiven
Roles: Fighter, Skirmisher
Region: Ionia
Faction: None
Resource: Flow (unique resource)
Attack Range: Melee

## Lore
Yasuo is a master swordsman of Ionia who manipulates wind with his blade. 
He was falsely accused of killing an elder and has lived as a wandering exile ever since.
He seeks redemption after learning the truth and reconnects with his brother Yone.

## Abilities
Steel Tempest (Q): Yasuo thrusts his sword forward, stacking toward a tornado.
Wind Wall (W): Yasuo creates a barrier that blocks enemy projectiles.
Sweeping Blade (E): Yasuo dashes through enemies, dealing magic damage.
Last Breath (R): Yasuo teleports to airborne enemies, suspending them and striking rapidly.
