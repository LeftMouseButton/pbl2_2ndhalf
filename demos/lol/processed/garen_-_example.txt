# Garen
Title: The Might of Demacia
Roles: Fighter, Tank
Region: Demacia
Faction: Dauntless Vanguard
Resource: None (uses no mana)
Attack Range: Melee

## Lore
Garen is a proud soldier of Demacia, dedicated to maintaining justice and order. 
He represents Demaciaâ€™s ideals of honor and courage. As a member of the Dauntless Vanguard 
and brother to Lux, he fights to protect his homeland from internal and external threats.

## Abilities
Decisive Strike (Q): Garen gains movement speed and his next attack silences the target.
Courage (W): Garen gains a shield and damage reduction.
Judgment (E): Garen rapidly spins with his sword, striking nearby enemies.
Demacian Justice (R): Garen executes a champion, dealing damage based on their missing health.

## Relationships
Garen is closely tied to Lux and the Demacian royal family. He often opposes Noxian champions such as Katarina.

## Moves (summary)
Garen uses simple but powerful melee abilities focused on durability and execution.
