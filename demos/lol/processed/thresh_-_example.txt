# Thresh
Title: The Chain Warden
Roles: Support, Control
Region: Shadow Isles
Faction: None (Undead)
Resource: Mana
Attack Range: 450

## Lore
Thresh is a spectral jailer from the Shadow Isles who delights in torment. 
Once a custodian of arcane secrets, he became twisted by the Ruination. 
He traps souls to prolong his own existence and manipulates victims with cruel cunning.

## Abilities
Death Sentence (Q): Thresh throws his scythe, pulling himself to enemies he hooks.
Dark Passage (W): Thresh throws a lantern that shields allies and allows them to dash to him.
Flay (E): Thresh sweeps his chain, knocking enemies in a direction.
The Box (R): Thresh creates a prison of spectral walls that damage and slow enemies.

## Relationships
Thresh has strong lore connections to Lucian and Senna, whose souls he once tormented.

## Moves (summary)
Thresh uses utility and crowd control abilities to protect allies and manipulate enemies.
