# Ahri
Title: The Nine-Tailed Fox
Roles: Mage, Assassin
Region: Ionia
Faction: Vastaya
Resource: Mana
Attack Range: 550

## Lore
Ahri is a Vastayan fox spirit with the ability to manipulate emotions and steal life essence. 
She once consumed the life force of mortals, but now strives to understand her origins and find a new path.

## Abilities
Orb of Deception (Q): Ahri throws an orb that travels out and back, dealing magic damage and then true damage.
Fox-Fire (W): Three spectral flames circle Ahri and automatically target nearby enemies.
Charm (E): Ahri blows a kiss that charms an enemy, causing them to walk harmlessly toward her.
Spirit Rush (R): Ahri dashes forward up to three times, firing energy bolts at nearby enemies.

## Relationships
Ahri has lore connections to Syndra, Yone, and Yasuo through Ionian history.

## Moves (summary)
Ahri uses long-range magic abilities focused on burst damage and mobility.
