# Pikachu
Number: 25
Types: Electric
Abilities: Static
Hidden Ability: Lightning Rod

## Pok√©dex Entries
Pikachu stores electricity in its cheeks. It releases electric discharges when threatened or excited. Pikachu live in small groups and communicate by tail signals.

## Evolution
- Evolves from Pichu when leveled up with high friendship.
- Evolves into Raichu when exposed to a Thunder Stone.
- Evolves into Alolan Raichu when exposed to a Thunder Stone in the Alola region.

## Moves (summary)
Pikachu learns many Electric-type attacks, including Thunder Shock and Thunderbolt, and can learn a wide variety of TMs.
