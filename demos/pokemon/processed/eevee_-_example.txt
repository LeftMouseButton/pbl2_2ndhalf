
# Eevee
Number: 133
Types: Normal
Abilities: Run Away, Adaptability
Hidden Ability: Anticipation

## Pok√©dex Entries
Eevee has an irregular genetic code that allows it to evolve into many different forms. Its unstable structure enables it to adapt to various environments.

## Evolution
- Evolves into Vaporeon when exposed to a Water Stone.
- Evolves into Jolteon when exposed to a Thunder Stone.
- Evolves into Flareon when exposed to a Fire Stone.
- Evolves into Espeon when leveled up during the day with high friendship.
- Evolves into Umbreon when leveled up at night with high friendship.
- Evolves into Leafeon when leveled up near a Moss Rock.
- Evolves into Glaceon when leveled up near an Ice Rock.
- Evolves into Sylveon when leveled up knowing a Fairy-type move with high affection.

## Moves (summary)
Eevee learns a variety of Normal-type moves and can learn numerous other moves via TMs and breeding.
